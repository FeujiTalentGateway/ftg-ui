How can I reschedule my exam date or time?

Most exam portals allow you to reschedule your exam by logging into your account and accessing the exam scheduling section. Follow the prompts to select a new date and time based on availability. Be mindful that rescheduling may incur additional fees.

How long are exam scores valid?

The validity period of exam scores varies depending on the exam and the organization administering it. Check the exam guidelines or contact the exam provider for information on score validity.
